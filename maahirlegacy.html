Product Details

Indulge in the unapologetic allure of Afnan Supremacy Not Only Intense, a fragrance that exudes confidence and sophistication. This bold scent begins with a tart and fruity top note combination of black currant and apple, infused with the bright and uplifting quality of bergamot.

As the fragrance unfolds, it reveals a complex and earthy heart, featuring the mossy, woody nuances of oakmoss, the grounding properties of patchouli, and the subtle floral charm of lavender. The dry down is a masterclass in sensuality, with the warmth and depth of ambergris mingling with the seductive allure of musk and the subtle spice of saffron. The result is a scent that is both intense and alluring, perfect for making a statement.

Afnan Supremacy Not Only Intense boasts impressive longevity, lingering on the skin for hours and leaving behind a trail that is both bold and irresistible. Experience this unapologetic fragrance with a sample and let it become your signature for nights filled with drama and sophistication.
